B.Sc. Final Project - Physical and Cognitive Trainer based on Kinect and Arduino

As part of my B.Sc. final project I developed a Physical and Cognitive Trainer for adults, in order to improve their physical and cognitive health and abilities.

The project focuses on the user's ability to imitate several hand gestures coded in the game. However, instead of clicking on buttons, The Kinect camera records the user's movements and decides whether he is doing the right gesture. The system is based on Kinect and Arduino and was developed by C# and Arduino IDE.
